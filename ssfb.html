OVERVIEW

Crafting new identity

Designed as the IAT 238 (Foundations for Designing Interactions) final project, our team of 5 was tasked with creating a microsite with unique visual identity for the SSFB festival.

MISSION

How can we create a digital experience that captures the essence of the SSFB festival, while fostering a deeper connection between artists and attendees?

SOLUTION

Focus on artist community

To build excitement for the festival, we designed the site as a pre-festival phase, creating anticipation and curiosity through artist focused content and interactive exploration.

MY CONTRIBUTIONS

Below are my contributions to the research & design :)

RESEARCH

Understanding the client

We researched the festivals history, purpose, and values to understand what makes it unique and appealing to audiences.




Design Techniques Inspired by Carson

During the weeks leading up to the final microsite, we researched graphic designer David Carson's experimental design techniques and explored various poster and microsite directions, giving form for the final site.




Experimentation & Iteration

During the weeks leading up to the final microsite, we were assigned with designing iterative posters and microsites, forcing us to think laterally, critically, and creatively.

KEY FLOWS

Exploratory artist discovery

Interactive elements give teasers to artist pages, while the pages themselves have a lot of super cool fun exciting details about the artist.




Simplified ticket flow

After excitement is built, we wanted to maintain that engagement throughout the ticket pages, guiding more exciting visual designs and music playing throughout.

DECISIONS

Building an experimental identity

Utilizing bold typography, dither textures, and overlays created a new identity for the festival.




Music player UI

Designed a music player that allows users to listen through individual artists top tracks, teasing what could be experienced live at the festival. This UI underwent iterations to push focus onto one song at a time.




Artist catalogue

Designed and prototyped a more functional artist catalogue for users to review the complete festival lineup.
